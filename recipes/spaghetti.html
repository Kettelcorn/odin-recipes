Spaghetti Recipe

This Spaghetti is a very tasty recipe. It is also very easy to make!

Makes 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Spaghetti noodles
  • 1 Jar Marinara Sauce
  • 1 lb Italian Sausage
  • Olive Oil
    • a
  1. Place pot of water on stove and heat to boiling, then place noodles in the pot
  2. Spread olive oil on pan and cook sausage at medium heat until well cooked
  3. Drain noodles, and mix them with marinara sauce and sausage
